**notes:**

- This is a passage from the New Testament (John 16:33). “ἐν ἐμοὶ εἰρήνην ἔχητε” is best taken as receiving/holding peace in relationship with Jesus (“in me”). “ἐν τῷ κόσμῳ θλῖψιν ἔχετε” is emphatic: tribulation is expected “in the world,” but believers are encouraged by the contrasting promise that Jesus has “conquered/overcome” the world.
