## Translation Analysis

**translation:**

- And Salmon begot Boaz by Rahab; and Boaz begot Obed by Ruth; and Obed begot Jesse.

| translation | notes |

**notes:**

- This is a standard biblical genealogical formula in Greek: “A begot B from/through C.” The names correspond to figures in the Matthew/Old Testament lineage tradition (Rahab, Ruth, Jesse). The English “begot” is archaic but conveys the original meaning precisely.
