**notes:**

- This is a standard genealogy formula in Koine Greek: “δὲ” often functions as a connective “and,” and “ἐγέννησεν” is “begot” (i.e., fathered). The names are transliterated as Eliud, Eleazar, Matthan, and Jacob.
