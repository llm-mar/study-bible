**translation:**

**notes:**

- This is a genealogical statement (as in Matthew’s Gospel). ἐξ ἧς literally means “from whom,” referring to Mary (ἧς = “her/whom”). The phrase ὁ λεγόμενος Χριστός means “who is called Christ,” with Χριστός functioning as the title/name “Christ” (the Anointed/Messiah).
